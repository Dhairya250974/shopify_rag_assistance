Customer segmentation

Customer segments are dynamic, rule-based customer lists that let you group together customers who have similar characteristics. You can build customer segments that meet your business needs by using ShopifyQL to combine filter names, operators, and values into a query. After you create a customer segment, customers are automatically added or removed to the segment list based on whether they meet the segment criteria.

Your customer segments can help you to communicate effectively by sending the right message to the right customers at the right time. You can use customer segmentation to benefit your store in the following ways:
