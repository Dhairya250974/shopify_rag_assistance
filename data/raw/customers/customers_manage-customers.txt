You might have two or more customer profiles that refer to one customer. In this case, you can merge two profiles together, combining them into one profile. Before you complete the merge, you have the opportunity to review and select what information the merged profile will have.

The profile that remains after a merge depends on the contact information that's kept on the merged profile. If only one profile has an email address, then that profile remains. If both profiles have email addresses, then the profile associated with the email address kept on the merged profile remains. If neither profile has an email address, then the profile that you select under Find a customer to merge with remains.

For example, a customer has 2 profiles in your store, one with the email address customer-old@example.com and another with customer-new@example.com. You open the profile for customer-old@example.com, click Merge customer, and then select customer-new@example.com under Find a customer to merge with. Before you confirm the merge, review the Merged customer section and choose which contact details to keep. If the merged profile keeps customer-new@example.com, then the profile associated with customer-new@example.com remains.

If a customer asks you to erase their personal data, then you can submit a data erasure request. The customer's personal details - such as their name, email, phone, and address - are removed from Shopify’s records, and the same request is sent to any apps and sales channels you’ve installed. Erasing personal data is different from deleting a customer profile. When you request to erase personal data, Shopify redacts information such as the customer’s name and address, but the profile and order history remain in your admin. For detailed steps, refer to Processing customer data requests.

Deleting a customer profile can't be undone.

You can delete individual customer profiles. Alternatively, you can delete multiple customer profiles in bulk.

When a customer profile is deleted, the customer’s personal data, such as their name and address, is also redacted from Shopify's records.
