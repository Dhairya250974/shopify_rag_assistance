There are several Shopify pricing plans with competitive rates that can fit your business needs.

If you're unsure about which pricing plan to choose, then register for a free trial to learn how Shopify's features can help you to sell your products and build your business. You can always upgrade your plan to add new features and functionality to your store.

If you plan to sell in person, then learn how Shopify POS features and hardware can support your retail business.
