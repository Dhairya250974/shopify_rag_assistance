# Inventory transfers and shipments

You can create inventory transfers to move and track inventory between your store locations, or to and from external locations such as suppliers. You might need to move items to replenish stock at another location, fulfill customer orders, or transfer inventory between different branches of your business. When you process a transfer, shipments are automatically created and managed for you.

The following video covers how to create and manage transfers in your Shopify admin, fulfill and receive them in Shopify POS, and use Sidekick to analyze stock balances and create transfers automatically.

To see more videos, visit our YouTube channel.

You can also view and manage inventory transfers created by third-party apps from the Transfers page of your Shopify admin.

You can create a custom report to view inventory changes from transfers across your locations.
